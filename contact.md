### Engagement Framework

<div style="background: var(--color-bg-alt); padding: var(--space-xl); border-radius: var(--radius-lg); margin-bottom: var(--space-xl);">

#### 1. Discovery (Complimentary)
30-minute research conversation to assess alignment, scope, and feasibility.

**Output:** Preliminary assessment with recommended engagement tier.

</div>

<div style="background: var(--color-bg-alt); padding: var(--space-xl); border-radius: var(--radius-lg); margin-bottom: var(--space-xl);">

#### 2. Assessment (2-4 weeks)
Formal engagement: knowledge graph construction, maturity scoring, gap analysis, and roadmap.

**Output:** ArcadeDB graph export, GEO/AEO/AIO scorecard, prioritized remediation plan.

[See methodology →](/case-studies/)

</div>

<div style="background: var(--color-bg-alt); padding: var(--space-xl); border-radius: var(--radius-lg);">

#### 3. Engineering (4-12 weeks)
Architecture, implementation, and deployment of target-state infrastructure.

**Output:** Production artifacts — OPA policies, capability registries, Function Calling schemas, Schema.org entity frameworks, knowledge graph integrations.

</div>
