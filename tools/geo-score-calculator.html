About This Tool

The GEO Score Calculator implements a probabilistic model estimating the likelihood that a document will be cited by transformer-based RAG pipelines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude). The model is calibrated against our internal validation set of 1,200 documents across 5 generative engines, achieving AUC-ROC of 0.82. Weights and parameters are continuously refined as new citation data is collected. This is an open research tool — results are estimates, not guarantees. v0.1.0
