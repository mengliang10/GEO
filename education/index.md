## Learning Paths

### 🟢 **GEO Practitioner Path**
Learn the fundamentals of Generative Engine Optimization, from entity modeling to citation optimization.

**Topics:** Entity frameworks, structured data, topical authority, AI search behavior, citation analysis

### 🔵 **AEO Architect Path**
Master the art of building machine-readable digital ecosystems for the age of agentic AI.

**Topics:** Knowledge graphs, Schema.org/JSON-LD, agentic commerce, API-first content, LLM data readiness

### 🟣 **AIO Strategist Path**
Become an expert in AI-powered martech strategy, from data architecture to governance frameworks.

**Topics:** Composable CDP, AI governance, predictive analytics, martech modernization, FinOps for AI
